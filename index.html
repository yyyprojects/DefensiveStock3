What is a Defensive Stock?

This section introduces the fundamental concept of defensive stocks. Think of them as the steady, reliable players in your investment portfolio. They belong to companies that provide essential goods and services people need regardless of how the economy is doing‚Äîlike food, electricity, and healthcare. Because of this consistent demand, their performance tends to be stable, offering a shield for your investments during uncertain times.
